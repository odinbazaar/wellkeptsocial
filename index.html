Fair questions

Do I need to provide photos or content?

It helps, but it's not required. Photos of your team and your work make posts stronger — send them whenever you have them. When you don't, we design posts from your services, your story, and what makes your business different.

Which platforms do you manage?

Instagram and Facebook — the two places local customers actually check before they call. Keeping those two genuinely alive beats being half-present on five platforms.

Who approves what gets posted?

You do. Every post is sent to you for approval before it's published. Nothing goes out under your business's name without your OK.

Is there a contract or setup fee?

No and no. It's a flat $297 a month, month to month. If it's not working for you, cancel with a single email — no exit calls, no fine print.

Our account has been dead for a year. Is that a problem?

It's the most common starting point we see, and it's fixable. A steady month of good posts reads as "under new management" — in the best way. The sooner the feed restarts, the sooner it stops costing you customers.
